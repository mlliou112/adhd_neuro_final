Other Resources

  • Center of Disease Control

    The CDC conducts ongoing research and collects data about ADHD. The CDC also has one of the most comprehensive sites for all information about ADHD, as well as the most up to date information about organizations and news reports related to ADHD.

    Learn More...

  • National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH)

    The NIMH is the lead federal agency for research on mental disorders through basic and clinical research. Their website about ADHD contains straightforward but detailed information about all aspects of ADHD.

    Learn More...

  • WebMD-ADHD

    WebMD has compiled a number of different sources on ADHD that you may find helpful. Much of the information is suited for a quick overview of ADHD, but also has information about related disorders and ADHD communities.

    Learn More...

  • Children and Adults with ADHD (CHADD)

    CHADD is a non-profit organization solely dedicated to improving the lives of individuals with ADHD. The organization has put together a number of resources that you may find helpful if dealing with ADHD.

    Learn More...
